The founder

I'm Jesse.

I've recently survived two under two (they're 2 and 3 now). It was a chapter. Therapy is ongoing.

One Monday, I added up how long I'd spent planning that week's dinners. An hour and a half. For five meals. Checking what we had, writing the list, second-guessing, Googling "easy dinners that aren't the same three things I rotate every week." Every single Monday.

I built Stacy's Mom to take it off my plate, and if I'm being totally honest, to banish my decision fatigue. To make balanced dinners that feel intentional, not put together at 5.48pm. A few friends asked for access. Then their friends. And here we are.

Built with love, from one completely done woman to another.
